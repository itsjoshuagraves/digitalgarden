## Assets Designers Commonly Need

- Brand assets
    - Logos (in .eps, .ai, or .svg preferred)
    - Typefaces
    - Brand colors
    - Photography
- Other miscellaneous items
    - Account creation (e.g. Webflow)
    - Content
        - We won’t need it all initially, we’ll give you space to fill in what you need
