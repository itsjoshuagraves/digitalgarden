    Workshop Facilitation Kit

    Here’s a shopping list if you’re doing in-person workshop facilitation with people.

    If you’re going in-person to facilitate with people, here are some helpful tools. Note the clock timer, that’s crucial in keeping people on task.

    1. Sticky notes (a lot of colors, 4 ideally)
    2. Sticky pad (24x36)
    3. Dots
    4. Sharpies (thick means less obsession over details)
    5. Clock timer
    6. Pens
    7. Painters’ tape
    8. Something to carry it in
